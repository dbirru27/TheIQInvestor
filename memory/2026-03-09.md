## Rotation Score Formula (rotation_catcher.py)
- 6 weighted signals (0-100 composite):
  - RS divergence (22%) — price vs SPY/QQQ weekly ✅ backtestable
  - Earnings revisions (17%) — forward EPS estimates ❌ NOT backtestable (yfinance live only)
  - Valuation gap (17%) — PE/PEG vs sector ❌ partially backtestable (no historical forward PE)
  - Stage breakout (22%) — price vs MAs, MA slope ✅ backtestable
  - Volume accumulation (11%) — volume patterns ✅ backtestable
  - Sector momentum (11%) — sector ETF vs SPY ✅ backtestable
- Convergence bonus: +4/7/10 if 3/4/5+ signals score ≥60
- ~83% backtestable (66% pure price/volume, ~17% from SEC EDGAR fundamentals)
- EWROS already captures the backtestable portion more cleanly
